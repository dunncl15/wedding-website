Charlie & Kylie's Wedding

09-09-17

It's Time to Eat, Drink & Be Married!

Our Story

Kylie and Charlie met in 2007 during their senior year of high school through a group of mutual friends. Little did they know, what started as a young romance would turn into a lifelong friendship and eventually marriage. Fondly known as "Chylie" by their closest friends, the two have been by each others' sides for the past 10 years. Much has changed over their time together, they migrated west to Denver, CO, purchased a home together, and have traveled the world. They are incredibly humbled and grateful to spend their wedding day surrounded by friends and family in Vermont where it all began.

Venue

The Barn at Boyden Farm

44 VT-104, Cambridge, VT 05444 - View Map

4:00 PM - Ceremony

The ceremony will be held outside at Boyden Farm promptly at 4:00 PM. Overnight parking is available onsite. Please arrive 15 minutes early.

5:00 PM - Cocktail Hour

The cocktail hour will immediately follow the ceremony and will be held outside on the barn patio. Please enjoy a few cocktails, hor d'oeurves, and lawn games before dinner.

6:00 PM - Dinner and Dancing

Please join us for a three course dinner prepared by the local restaurant 158 Main inside the beautiful Boyden Farm Barn.

11:00 PM - After Party

The party at Boyden Farm will conclude by 11pm. We'll be heading up the road to the Brewster River Pub to continue the celebration. There will be a shuttle provided from the venue to the bar.

Accommodations

Smuggler's Notch Resort

4323 Vermont 108 S, Jeffersonsville, VT 05464
(800) 521-0536

This location is 15 minutes away from the Barn at Boyden Farm. Attendees will have the opportunity to share multi-bedroom condos. The condos will be placed all in one communal location. Smugglers' Notch booking includes unlimited use of pools, basketball, shuffleboard and resort activities. There are also hiking and fitness programs, zip lining, and other programs.

There are many options for all groupings. Choose to stay on your own in a studio or one-bedroom or bunk in a 3 or 4 bedroom condo with other friends and family. To book, please call or email Ben Childs at the number and email listed, be sure to mention that you are part of the McLane/Dunn Wedding.
